B.Ed Programme

About the Programme

B.Ed. is a two years program that is designed by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University-the affiliating University.


Intake

Programme intake is -100 (2units)


Eligibility Criteria

(1) For Graduates: - Candidates with at least 50% either in Bachelor degree and /or in the master’s degree in Science, Social Science and Humanities

OR

Bachelor in Engineering or Technology with  specialization in Science and Mathematica. With 55% marks or any other qualification equivalent thereto.

Note:

Candidates having fulfilled the eligibility conditions, as mentioned above, may opt for any two teaching methodology Subjects as per details given below for graduates and post gradates.

Graduate: Candidates must have passed at least two subjects out of the teaching methodology subjects as mentioned below:

(i)  English

(ii)  Hindi

(iii)  Urdu

(iv)  Punjabi

(v)  Sanskrit

(vi) Mathematics

(vii) Integrated Science

(viii) Social Science

Note:

(i) Institute shall abide strictly to the NCTE norms for admission in B.Ed.

(ii) Candidates are advised not to choose two languages as teaching methodology subjects

(iii) The choice of the teaching Methodology shall be based on the subjects studied by the Graduation level.

(iv) The candidate must have passed at least two subjects of the list of the teaching methodology subjects as mentioned above.

(v) The second teaching subject can be chosen either from the main subject or subsidiary subject passed by the graduation level.

(vi) The candidates who has studied either Political Science /History/ Geography/ Economics /Psychology will be eligible to take social Science as one of the teaching subject.

(II) Post Graduate: Candidates must have passed at least two subjects out of the following list of the teaching methodology subjects as mentioned below:

(i)  English

(ii)  Hindi

(iii)  Urdu

(iv)  Punjabi

(v)  Sanskrit

(vi) Mathematics

(vii) Biology

(viii) Physics

(ix) Chemistry

(x) Accountancy

(xi) Business Studies

(xii) Political science

(xiii) History

(xiv) Geography

(xv) Economics

(xvi) Home Science

Note:

(i)Institute shall abide strictly to the NCTE norms for admission in B.E.D.

(ii) Candidates are advised not to choose two language as teaching methodology subject

(iii) The choice of the teaching methodology shall be based on the subject studied by the candidate at the post-Graduation live.

(iv) The candidate must have passed at least two subject out of the teaching methodology subject as mentioned above. Candidates will be allotted teaching subjects from the subjects listed above

(v) The first teaching subject candidate be chosen the subject passed by the candidate post-Graduation level.

(vi) The second Teaching Subject can be chosen either form the main subject or Subsidiary subjects passed by the candidate at Graduation level.

Note:

(i)The candidate who has passed a paper either in Botany /Zoology/Biotechnology/ Life Science will be eligible to take Biology as one of the teaching subject.

(ii) The second Teaching Subject can be chosen either form the main subject or Subsidiary subjects passed by the candidate at Graduation level.

Admission Procedure

The admission are carried out strictly on merit basis through a Common Entrance Test (CET). conducted by GGIP University. The students are admitted to the institute of their choice through a central counselling conducted by the University. For more detail.  Visit www.ipu.ac.in
